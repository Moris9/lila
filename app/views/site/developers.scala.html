Embed lichess in your site

Do you have a chess website, and want to add a playable live chess section?
Add the following line to your site html: 

<script src="http://en.lichess.org/embed?w=820h=650"></script>

The w and h parameters control the width and height of the chess window.



Embed the Chess TV in your site

Do you have a chess website, and want to add a live chess TV?
Add the following line to your site html: 

<iframe frameBorder='0' style='width: 226px; height: 266px'
src="http://en.lichess.org/embed/tv?theme=brown&bg=light"></iframe>

Parameters:

  • - theme: Controls the board theme. Available themes: brown, blue, green, purple, yellow, grey, wood, canvas, leather.
  • - bg: Controls the background color. Available backgrounds: light, dark.


Example with dark background and wood theme:

<iframe frameBorder='0' style='width: 226px; height: 266px' 
src="http://en.lichess.org/embed/tv?theme=wood&bg=dark"></iframe>
